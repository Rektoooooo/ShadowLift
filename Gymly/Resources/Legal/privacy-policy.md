## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data Lives

**Local Storage:**
- All workout data is primarily stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
- Data is encrypted using iOS built-in encryption
- No external database unless you enable iCloud sync

**iCloud Storage (Optional):**
- When enabled, data syncs to your private iCloud account
- Uses Apple's end-to-end encryption
- Only accessible with your Apple ID credentials
- We cannot access your iCloud data

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** All sensitive data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- **Secure Authentication:** Apple Sign In uses secure OAuth tokens
- **No Plain Text Passwords:** We don't store passwords (handled by Apple)
- **Access Controls:** Strict internal access policies

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data permanently deleted within 30 days of deletion request
- **Backup Data:** May be retained in backups for up to 90 days

## Your Rights and Choices

You have significant control over your data.

### Access Your Data

You can access all your data at any time within the app:
- View workout history in Calendar
- Export data (Premium feature)
- Review profile information in Settings

**Data Export:** Email sebastian.kucera@icloud.com to request a complete copy of your data in machine-readable format (JSON).

### Delete Your Data

**Within the App:**
- Delete individual workouts from Calendar
- Remove progress photos
- Clear exercise history

**Delete Your Account:**
- Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
- Or email sebastian.kucera@icloud.com with subject "Delete My Account"
- All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

### Control Data Syncing

- **iCloud:** Toggle on/off in Settings → Connections
- **Apple Health:** Manage permissions in iOS Settings → Health → Apps → ShadowLift

### GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you're in the European Union, you have additional rights:
- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format

Email sebastian.kucera@icloud.com to exercise these rights.

### CCPA Rights (California Users)

California residents have rights under CCPA:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Access personal information (twice per year, free)
- Delete personal information
- Opt-out of sale of personal information (we don't sell data)

Email sebastian.kucera@icloud.com with subject "CCPA Request" to exercise these rights.
