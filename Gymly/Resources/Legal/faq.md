## Getting Started

### How do I create my first workout split?

1. Tap **Settings** tab ‚Üí **Workout Splits**
2. Tap the **+** button in the top right
3. Choose **Create Custom Split** or select a **Premium Template** (PPL, PHAT, etc.)
4. Name your split (e.g., "Push Pull Legs")
5. Add days to your split (e.g., "Push Day", "Pull Day", "Legs Day")
6. Add exercises to each day
7. Tap **Activate Split** to start tracking

### How do I log my first workout?

1. Make sure you have an active split
2. Go to the **Routine** tab
3. You'll see today's workout based on your split
4. Tap **+** on an exercise to add a set
5. Enter your weight and reps
6. Mark the set as complete by tapping the checkmark
7. Complete all sets for all exercises
8. ShadowLift automatically saves your progress!

### What's the difference between a Split and a Day?

- **Split**: Your overall workout program (e.g., "Push Pull Legs" 6-day split)
- **Day**: Individual workout within your split (e.g., "Push Day" with chest, shoulders, triceps)

You create a split once, and it contains multiple days that cycle through automatically.

## Workout Tracking

### How do I add a new exercise?

**During a workout:**
1. Go to **Routine** tab
2. Scroll to the bottom of your exercises
3. Tap **Add Exercise**
4. Search or browse the exercise library
5. Select your exercise and configure sets/reps
6. Start logging!

**To your split:**
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Workout Splits**
2. Select your active split
3. Choose the day you want to edit
4. Tap **+ Add Exercise**

### Can I create custom exercises?

Not yet! Currently ShadowLift uses a curated exercise library of 200+ exercises. Custom exercise creation is planned for a future update.

**Workaround**: Find a similar exercise in the library and add notes to specify your variation.

### How do I edit my rep goals?

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Workout Splits**
2. Select your split and day
3. Tap on the exercise you want to edit
4. Update the **Rep Goal** field
5. Tap **Save**

**Rep Goal Format Examples:**
- `8-12` (rep range)
- `5x5` (sets x reps)
- `AMRAP` (as many reps as possible)
- `20` (exact rep target)

### What do the different set types mean?

- **Normal Set**: Regular working set
- **Warm-up Set**: Lower weight, used to prepare muscles (not counted in volume stats)
- **Failure Set**: Trained to muscular failure
- **Drop Set**: Reduce weight mid-set and continue
- **Rest-Pause**: Brief pause (10-15s) then continue
- **Bodyweight**: No additional weight (uses bodyweight only)

**To mark a set type:**
Tap the set indicator icons when logging a set.

### How does ShadowLift track my progress?

ShadowLift tracks several metrics automatically:
- **Volume**: Total weight lifted (sets √ó reps √ó weight)
- **Personal Records (PRs)**: Your best performance for each exercise (Premium)
- **Workout Frequency**: Days per week you train
- **Muscle Group Balance**: Radar chart showing training distribution
- **Streak**: Consecutive workout days

View your progress in:
- **Profile** tab ‚Üí Progress graphs
- **Calendar** tab ‚Üí Historical workouts
- **AI Summary** (iOS 18.1+, Premium) ‚Üí Weekly insights

## Syncing & Data

### How does iCloud sync work?

**When enabled:**
- All workout data syncs across your Apple devices
- Progress photos sync (if Premium)
- User profile syncs
- Workout splits sync

**When disabled:**
- Data stays local on your device only
- No cross-device access
- No data backup (unless you backup your iPhone)

**To enable/disable:**
Settings ‚Üí Connections ‚Üí iCloud Sync (toggle on/off)

### Why isn't my data syncing?

**Common issues:**

1. **iCloud not enabled in ShadowLift**
   - Settings ‚Üí Connections ‚Üí Enable iCloud Sync

2. **Signed out of iCloud**
   - iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Sign in

3. **Poor network connection**
   - ShadowLift pauses sync on poor cellular to prevent lag
   - Connect to WiFi for faster sync

4. **iCloud storage full**
   - iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí iCloud ‚Üí Manage Storage
   - Free up space or upgrade plan

5. **Background app refresh disabled**
   - iPhone Settings ‚Üí ShadowLift ‚Üí Enable Background App Refresh

### Does ShadowLift work offline?

**Yes!** ShadowLift is designed for offline-first operation:

- ‚úÖ Log workouts without internet

## Apple Health Integration

### How do I connect Apple Health?

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Connections**
2. Tap **Connect to Health**
3. Review permissions and tap **Allow**
4. Choose what data to sync:
   - ‚úÖ Body Weight (read & write)
   - ‚úÖ Height (read)
   - ‚úÖ Workouts (write)

### What data does ShadowLift sync with Health?

**ShadowLift writes:**
- Workout sessions (duration, start/end time)
- Body weight updates

**ShadowLift reads:**
- Body weight (for charts and BMI)
- Height (for BMI calculation)
- Date of birth (for age)

### Why isn't my weight syncing from Health?

**Check these:**

1. **Health permissions granted**
   - iPhone Settings ‚Üí Health ‚Üí Data Access & Devices ‚Üí ShadowLift
   - Ensure "Weight" is enabled

2. **Weight data exists in Health**
   - Open Health app ‚Üí Browse ‚Üí Body Measurements ‚Üí Weight
   - Add a weight entry if none exists

3. **Reconnect Health**
   - ShadowLift Settings ‚Üí Connections ‚Üí Disconnect Health
   - Then reconnect and grant permissions again

### Can I stop syncing with Health?

**Yes:**

Settings ‚Üí Connections ‚Üí Health Integration ‚Üí Disconnect

**Note**: This stops future syncing but doesn't delete existing data from Health app. To delete Health data, use the Health app directly.

## Troubleshooting

### The app is lagging during my workout. Why?

This was a known issue (the "gym lag" problem) that has been fixed in recent updates. If you're still experiencing lag:

**Try these fixes:**

1. **Update to latest version**
   - App Store ‚Üí Updates ‚Üí Update ShadowLift

2. **Disable iCloud sync during workouts**
   - Settings ‚Üí Connections ‚Üí Disable iCloud Sync
   - Re-enable after workout for WiFi

3. **Close background apps**
   - Swipe up from home screen ‚Üí Close unused apps

4. **Restart your iPhone**
   - Hold power button ‚Üí Slide to power off ‚Üí Turn back on

5. **Check available storage**
   - iPhone Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí iPhone Storage
   - Need at least 1GB free space

If lag persists, contact support@gymly.app with your iPhone model and iOS version.

### My workout streak is wrong. How do I fix it?

**Common reasons:**

1. **Rest days configured incorrectly**
   - Settings ‚Üí Profile ‚Üí Edit Profile ‚Üí Rest Days Per Week
   - Set to your actual rest days (default: 2)

2. **Workout logged on wrong date**
   - Calendar tab ‚Üí Find incorrect workout ‚Üí Delete or edit date

3. **Streak calculation logic**
   - ShadowLift allows up to `1 + rest days` gap before breaking
   - Example: 2 rest days = 3 day max gap

**To manually adjust:**
Contact support@gymly.app if your streak needs correction.

### I can't find a specific exercise. What do I do?

**Search tips:**
- Try alternative names (e.g., "bench press" vs "barbell bench")
- Use muscle group filter in exercise picker
- Search by equipment type

**Exercise not in library?**
Email hello@gymly.app with the exercise name and we'll consider adding it in future updates.

**Workaround**: Use a similar exercise and add notes to specify your variation.

### The app crashed. Will I lose my workout?

**No!** ShadowLift auto-saves after every set you log.

**If app crashes during workout:**
1. Reopen ShadowLift
2. Go to Routine tab
3. Your logged sets will still be there
4. Continue where you left off

**Data is saved to device immediately** - crashes don't cause data loss.

### How do I reset my password?

ShadowLift uses **Apple Sign In** which doesn't have passwords!

Your Apple ID credentials secure your account. To change your Apple ID password:

1. iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Password & Security
2. Tap **Change Password**
